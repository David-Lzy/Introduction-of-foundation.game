# 2026-07-04 Official social update: Star of the Guild and the July 7-13 Interstellar Migration wave

## Newly confirmed today

- The official **2026-06-21** post introduces a new weekly **Star of the Guild** feature for **Servers 1-16**.
  - The **Top 10 Commerce Guilds by Power** qualify every Monday at **00:00 UTC**.
  - Qualification rankings are **locked** once the weekly cutoff is reached.
  - Weekly rewards depend on overall **Commerce Guild** activity and performance.
  - The **Guild Leader** selects one special **Honorable Trader**.
  - Each **Commerce Guild** has its own Star lineup, and all guild members share the base rewards, while total rewards can rise with each Guild Star's performance.
- The official **2026-06-30 12:00 UTC** post confirms the next **Interstellar Migration** batch for **Servers 1089-1120**.
  - Migration Groups are **1089-1096**, **1097-1104**, **1105-1112**, and **1113-1120**.
  - The event schedule is **July 7 - July 13**.
  - **Migration Notice Period**: **July 7 - July 9**
  - **Migration Application Period**: **July 10 - July 11**
  - **Migration Day**: **July 12**
  - **Migration Showcase Period**: **July 13**

## What players / officers should do

- If your server is in **1-16**, track the Monday **00:00 UTC** qualification cutoff for **Star of the Guild** and do not leave weekly power pushes to the last minute.
- If you lead a **Commerce Guild**, decide early who should be the weekly **Honorable Trader** and make sure members understand that both shared guild output and individual Guild Star performance affect the final payout.
- Treat **Star of the Guild** as a weekly participation loop, not a one-time reward pop-up. Plan activity, spending, and timing around the lock point.
- If your server is in **1089-1120**, save the migration calendar now and make sure members know the difference between the **notice**, **application**, **migration**, and **showcase** windows.
- Do not rely on the older **Servers 1001-1024** migration note anymore if you are in the newer batch. The currently confirmed July wave is specifically for **1089-1120**.
