# 2026-05-14 Official Windows Launch & Login Rewards

## Official details confirmed today

- The official page now states that **Foundation: Galactic Frontier** is "Now officially live on Windows!" and provides an official Windows installer download entry.
- The page says players can claim a **Permanent Portrait Frame** by logging in on Windows.
- The same page also confirms extra rewards from accumulated sign-ins, but it does not publish the full reward table.

## What this means for players

- Anyone who wants the **Permanent Portrait Frame** needs at least one Windows login.
- Players planning to move part of their routine to PC now have an official Windows entry point instead of relying on emulator landing pages.
- The accumulated sign-in reward track is confirmed to exist, but the exact milestones and deadline still need to be checked in the in-game event panel.

## Recommended actions

- If you want the cosmetic reward, complete your first Windows login soon.
- If you want the extra accumulated sign-in rewards, check the in-game event panel before planning how many days to log in.
- Prefer the official Windows page over third-party mirrors or emulator promo pages.
