# 2026-07-03 Official social update: Guild Duel League test, roadmap checkpoint, and GALAXYWITHYOU

## Newly confirmed today

- The official **2026-06-30** maintenance notice says the **Commerce Guild Duel League** test has started.
  - The **test lasts 1 month**.
  - The regular League season is planned to last **6 months**.
  - **Commerce Guilds** now show **League Ranks**, and you earn **Rank Progress Points** after each **Commerce Guild Duel** settlement.
  - The overall leaderboard is now split between a **Days 1-5 Points Leaderboard** and a **Day 6 Leaderboard**.
- The same maintenance notice also adds several quality-of-life and planning tools:
  - a new **Event Calendar**
  - the **Appearance Shop**
  - a **Favorites** feature for coordinates, planets, special locations, and event targets
  - more **Encyclopedia Galactica** entries
- The official **Galaxy Progress Checkpoint Issue 2** update, posted on **2026-07-02**, gives the latest roadmap status:
  - **Server Transfer** has completed two test rounds and is now rolling out gradually
  - the fourth **Flagship**, **Gungnir**, becomes available on servers that have been open for **27 weeks**
  - **United Frontline** has finished its main development stage and is still being refined before a full re-release
  - **Guild Duel League** content is already in the latest update, but the full official release date is still pending
- The same roadmap update also lists the current development priorities:
  - **Season 2** is the highest priority and includes new story content, gameplay updates, and long-term progression improvements
  - **Shadowfront League** is in development
  - **Guild Ruins** and **Cross-Server Ruins** are being prepared as future **Commerce Guild** content
  - **Boss Rush Gameplay** is not a current priority
  - **Tower Defense** is still moving toward a permanent version
- A new official redeem code was posted in the same **2026-07-02** roadmap update:
  - **GALAXYWITHYOU**
  - valid until **2026-07-31 23:59 UTC**
  - the official post did **not** disclose the reward bundle

## What players / officers should do

- Treat the current **Commerce Guild Duel League** as a live test cycle, not a permanent settled ruleset. Track both the **1-month** test window and the later **6-month** season cadence.
- If your **Commerce Guild** pushes rankings seriously, check the new **League Rank** display and plan around the split between the **Days 1-5** leaderboard and the **Day 6** leaderboard.
- Start using the new **Event Calendar** to pre-plan event stamina, **Action Points (AP)** spending, and **Commerce Guild** coordination.
- Use **Favorites** on the world map for high-traffic rally points, repeated farming spots, and event objectives instead of relying on manual coordinate notes.
- Do not assume **Server Transfer** is globally open yet. Watch your own server rollout status first.
- If your server is nearing **27 weeks**, prepare materials and fleet plans in advance for **Gungnir** unlock timing.
- Do not plan around **United Frontline** as fully reopened yet; the official wording still says refinement is ongoing before full re-release.
- Redeem **GALAXYWITHYOU** before **2026-07-31 23:59 UTC** even though the reward contents have not been announced in the official post.
