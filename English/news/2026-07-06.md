# 2026-07-06 Official roadmap update: Season 2 target window and the next feature wave

## Newly confirmed today

- The official **2026-07-06** roadmap post sets the current target for **Season 2** at **August**.
  - If that target changes, the team says it will communicate an update **around the end of July**.
- The same roadmap post gives the clearest public outline so far for the next feature wave:
  - **Guild Ruins** and **Cross-Server Ruins** will expand **Ruin Raiders** with shared Guild spaces, easier high-value excavations, and cross-server matchmaking.
  - **Shadowfront League** is planned as a **64-team single-elimination tournament**.
  - The field will be built from the **Top 32 Guilds from every 8 servers**.
  - **Boss Rush Gameplay** is planned around consecutive boss fights.
- The post also repeats that all listed features are still **under development** and can change before they appear in game.

## What players / officers should do

- Treat **August** as the current planning target for **Season 2**, not a locked launch date. Watch for the official reconfirmation or delay note near the **end of July**.
- If you coordinate a strong **Commerce Guild**, start thinking about whether your roster can compete inside an **8-server block** if **Shadowfront League** keeps this format.
- Keep your **Ruin Raiders** setup flexible. The official wording suggests **Guild Ruins** and **Cross-Server Ruins** will put more weight on shared Guild spaces, higher-value excavation targets, and cross-server matching.
- Do not promise members that any of these systems are final yet. The roadmap explicitly says timing, details, and availability can still change before in-game confirmation.
