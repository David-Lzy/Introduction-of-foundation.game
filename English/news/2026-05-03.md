# 2026-05-03 App Store Version Notes

## Version 1.1.36 - current player-facing changes

- **Tender Letters** will be available soon. The activity asks players to collect fragrant flowers and unlock event rewards.
- **Jodie Béart's classic look** is returning as a free permanent unlock. Check the in-game event / appearance panel when the version is live on your server.
- **Top 100 Galactic Traders** has a revamped event interface with a progress bar animation when earning points, making milestone progress easier to track.
- **Paths to Dominance** now awards event points from PvP battles in Level 9 star systems during the event.

## Version 1.1.21 - systems worth checking in-game

- **United Frontline** is being tested on Servers 1001–1023. During this test, the **Ascendancy Shrine** stops spawning.
- **Prince's Decrees** have more uses. Buff effects and cooldowns no longer reset when **Paths to Dominance** begins, though bounty effects are still cleared on reset.
- In **Shadowfront**, when any Vault enters **Brief Awakening**, 6–9 resource nodes appear. Collecting them restores HP to the collecting Flagship, but only for Minor Damage.
- **Commerce Guild Duel** scoring now includes points for consuming **Weapon Prisms** on Tuesdays and Fridays. Weapon Energy Core points are reduced, and matchmaking better accounts for Champion Weapon unlock progress.
- **Champion Weapon** unlock timing changed: it unlocks on the Monday of Week 12 instead of exactly after 90 server days.
- **Dominion Warzone** received lower daily point requirements during Preparation Stage and a new **Warzone Mobilization** event that launches with Dominion Warzone.

## What officers should do

- Recheck event scoring pages before Paths to Dominance, Commerce Guild Duel, Shadowfront, and Dominion Warzone windows.
- For servers 1001–1023, confirm whether United Frontline is active before planning around Ascendancy Shrine spawns.
- Tell members to treat in-game event panels as the final authority because these are live-service version notes.
