# VS Guild Duel Strategy

VS (Guild Duel) is a weekly major event.
It's essentially a 6-day resource and execution rhythm competition between two guilds.

---

## I. Basic Rules

- Total duration: 6 days
- Different scoring focus each day
- Daily victories earn points
- Final winner determined by total score on Day 6
- Winner can upgrade reward pool tier
- New members typically cannot contribute points in first 24 hours

---

## II. Core Logic

VS is not about "last-minute scoring rushes," but rather:

> Stockpile in advance, release strategically on scoring days.

---

## III. Daily Focus

- **Day 1:** Building/shipbuilding speed-ups, trading, rallies
- **Day 2:** Champion level/star/shard investment and commissions
- **Day 3:** Research speed-ups, Deep Space Beacons, Echo Modules, Deep Space Echo
- **Day 4:** Flagship upgrades/advancement/blueprints/Prismatic Core consumption
- **Day 5:** Comprehensive development burst (building/research/shipbuilding/champions/rallies)
- **Day 6:** PvP combat (enemy fighter/flagship/core damage)

---

## IV. Rhythm Discipline

Common mistakes:

- Consuming all key resources during normal days
- Having no stockpile to release during VS

Correct approach:

- Daily restraint
- Concentrated release during scoring windows

Recommended stockpile priorities:

- Various speed-ups
- Galactic Coins
- Champion shards
- Deep Space Beacons
- Echo Modules
- Food

---

## V. Guild-Level Coordination

Leadership should:

1. Announce daily themes in advance
2. Coordinate resource release timing
3. Plan Day 6 combat rhythm
4. Avoid meaningless conflicts

---

## VI. One-Sentence Summary

VS tests resource management capability, not just power numbers.
