# Style Counters and Energy Resonance

This page only covers two things that quietly decide a lot of space-battle outcomes:

1. **style counters**
2. **energy resonance**

If your power looks close but fights still feel shaky, start here.

---

## 1. First split the rule correctly: style only matters in space battle

The GameWith page makes this very clear:

- **space battle** checks style (**Beam / Kinetic / Ion**)
- **ground battle** does **not** check style

So the practical rule is:

> care about style in space battle; do not break your best ground team just to force style matching.

---

## 2. The counter triangle

The counter order is:

- **Kinetic** → **Ion**
- **Ion** → **Beam**
- **Beam** → **Kinetic**

The page gives a direct payoff for attacking into advantage:

- **+5% damage**

That is not a huge number on paper,
but it often decides close fights.

---

## 3. Energy Resonance is the more reliable everyday buff

On top of counters, space teams also care about **Energy Resonance**.

Trigger condition:

- your **Flagship** and Heroes share the same style
- you field **2** or **3** Heroes of that same style

Effects listed on the source page:

- **2 matching Heroes**: ATK / DEF / INT **+10%**
- **3 matching Heroes**: ATK / DEF / INT **+20%**

So the default team-building rule is simple:

> for your main space team, start with the Flagship style and try to reach 2-hero or 3-hero Energy Resonance first.

---

## 4. When should you chase resonance first, and when should you chase counters first?

### Routine PvE and stable farming
Prioritize:

- keeping your main **Flagship** stable
- keeping **2-hero / 3-hero Energy Resonance** active

That is the easiest long-term setup to reuse every day.

### Once you already have multiple trained space teams
Then you can start switching into the favorable counter style for a specific target.

In other words:

> build a stable main team first, then optimize for counters.

Do not throw in an underbuilt off-style team just to chase that **5%** damage edge.

---

## 5. Two common mistakes

### Mistake 1: forcing style in ground battle
Wrong.
The source explicitly says ground battle does not use style.

Ground battle should care more about:

- your strongest 3 Heroes
- healing / shielding / grouping tools
- skill order

### Mistake 2: only looking at counters and ignoring resonance
Also wrong.

If you switch styles but lose:

- trained Heroes
- upgraded skills
- Energy Resonance

you often end up weaker overall.

---

## 6. Quick pre-fight checklist

Before a space battle, check these in order:

1. is this actually a **space battle**?
2. what style is my main **Flagship**?
3. can this team keep **2-hero / 3-hero Energy Resonance**?
4. if I have a spare trained team, can I also counter the target style?

If you get those 4 checks right, a lot of "same power, bad result" fights become much more stable.

---

## One-line takeaway

> **In space battle, respect style. In ground battle, ignore style. For your main space team, secure Energy Resonance before chasing counter swaps.**
