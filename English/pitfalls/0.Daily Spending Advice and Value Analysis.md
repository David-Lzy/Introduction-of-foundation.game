# Spending Advice and Value Analysis

This chapter is not about encouraging spending.

Rather:

> If you decide to spend, how to maximize value.

Free-to-play is viable, but progression will be slower.
Light spenders can significantly improve their experience.

---

## I. Spending Entry Points

Most spending options are located:

Top-right area of game interface.

Including:

* Champion Battle Pass
* Fund-type projects
* Black Card membership
* Weekly / Monthly cards
* Limited-time event packs

---

## II. Champion Battle Pass (Recommended: Beginners)

New Champion Battle Pass releases periodically.

Mechanism:

* Complete daily tasks to earn activity points
* Reach 40,000 activity points for free rewards
* Paid version unlocks additional reward track

---

### Recommended For

Recommended:

* New players
* Players below Core level 20

Reason:

* Low champion star levels benefit greatly from shards
* Fixed shard amounts provide fast progression

Not recommended:

* Mid-late game players above level 20

Reason:

* Champions near max level
* Limited shard value
* Excess water and metal have diminishing returns

---

## III. Counterattack Fund (Strongly Recommended)

Usually low-priced (e.g., $9.9 USD).

Returns:

* Large resource bundle
* Extremely high value

Classified as:

> One-time investment, high return project.

Recommended purchase.

---

## IV. Black Card Membership (Recommended)

Features include:

* Second research queue
* One free discount shop refresh per week
* Supreme Prayer Ship attempts +1
* Increased daily limits on certain activities

---

### Worth It?

Active players: Recommended
Inactive players: Case-by-case

If your research times reach days or weeks:

Second research queue provides significant value.

---

## V. Season Cumulative Spending (Not Recommended to Chase)

Example:

* 60-day cumulative spending rewards
* Special officers (e.g., Kofi Alansi)

Principle:

> Don't spend extra just to reach thresholds.

But if naturally approaching a key milestone, consider completing it.

---

## VI. Weekly and Monthly Cards

### Monthly Card (Higher Priority)

Contains:

* Galactic Coins
* Action Points
* Speed-ups
* Universal Credits

Recommended:

* All stage players

Galactic Coins will always be needed late-game.

---

### Weekly Card

Mainly provides:

* Water
* Metal
* Galactic Coins

Recommended:

* New players
* Resource-scarce stages

Limited budget:

> Monthly Card priority > Weekly Card

---

## VII. Star Flash Packs (New Champion Release)

When obtaining new champions:

Limited-time shard purchase options may appear.

Worth buying?

* Common gold (10 shards to summon): Skip
* High-threshold champions (40 shards to summon): Consider

Principle:

> Only special high-threshold champions worth considering.

Otherwise limited value.

---

## VIII. Activity Milestones (Strongly Recommended)

Location:

Bottom-left → Activity → Activity Milestones

Can unlock:

* Conquest Milestones
* Legendary Milestones

Recommended:

> Purchase "Conquest Milestones"

Better value, more concentrated resources.

Usually one-time investment with stable returns.

---

## IX. Comprehensive Priority Order

### One-Time Investments (Recommended)

1. Counterattack Fund
2. Activity Milestones (Conquest)

---

### Recurring Investments (Recommended)

1. Black Card Membership
2. Monthly Card
3. Weekly Card (if budget allows)
4. Champion Battle Pass (beginner stage)

---

## X. Core Principles

* Don't chase "cumulative spending" targets
* Don't stress over shards
* Don't impulse-buy for leaderboards
* Prioritize "long-term return projects"

---

## Summary

Three spending approaches:

1. Impulsive
2. Blind
3. Structured

This guide recommends:

> Structured spending.

Spend within your means.

Gaming is a long-term system.
Spending is a tool, not the goal.
