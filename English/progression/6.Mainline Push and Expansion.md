# Mainline Push and Expansion

This chapter covers three topics:

1. Main quests
2. Counterattack Operation
3. Planet expansion and map exploration

---

## I. Main Quests

Main quests are linear progression.

Features:

* Fixed sequence
* Choice differences have minimal impact
* One-time growth content

Conclusion:

> Just follow the flow, no need to overthink choices.

---

## II. Counterattack Operation

Location:

Top-left → Counterattack Operation

Essence:

> Power check system.

Enough power → Can clear
Insufficient power → Actually can't beat it

Not much technique involved.

---

### 1) Boss Every 10 Stages

* Boss appears every 10 stages
* Boss requires manual control

Other normal stages:

* Enter battle
* Click bottom-left "Auto"
* Exit interface and do other things

Good for beginners:

Power increases quickly,
come back later to auto-clear stages.

---

## III. Daily Activity Tasks (Must Do)

Location:

Bottom-left → Activity

Recommended targets:

* Daily activity to 140
* Weekly tasks to 330

Later rewards have diminishing returns, optional.

---

### Activity Interface

Left side shows:

* Story chapters
* Activity Milestones

---

### Activity Milestone Recommendations

Can unlock:

* Free rewards
* Paid rewards (Conquest Milestones / Legendary Milestones)

Recommendation:

> Prioritize "Conquest Milestones"
> Don't recommend "Legendary Milestones"

Better value.

(Detailed spending strategy in spending chapter.)

---

## IV. Hidden Progression: Map Expansion

Important progression method often overlooked by players.

---

### 1) Planet Unlocking

When a planet is controlled by Supreme Prayer:

Need to "lift blockade."

Recommendation:

> Start large-scale expansion around ship level 20.

Reason:

Sufficient power enables "crushing" battles.

Crushing ends battles instantly,
far more efficient than hard fighting.

---

### 2) Efficiency Tip: Flight Time Utilization

When flying to next planet (e.g., 30 seconds flight time):

Use this time to:

* Quickly complete daily tasks
* Claim system tasks
* Clear simple events

Avoid idle waiting.

---

### 3) When to Stop Pushing?

If:

* Current planet monster level too high
* Cannot crush
* Fighting is difficult

Recommend pausing expansion.

Continuing has poor cost-benefit,
wastes time.

---

## V. Map Exploration Rewards

Access:

Bottom-right → Map icon

Switch to sector map.

Check:

Exploration progress bar.

When exploration reaches 150:

Receive:

* Silver Champion shard rewards

Stable and easily overlooked source.

Recommendation:

> Each new sector, casually push exploration to 150.

---

## Summary

Mainline and expansion core points:

1. Main quests just follow through
2. Counterattack Operation depends on power
3. Daily activity must complete fully
4. Expansion after power enables crushing
5. Exploration 150 rewards must claim

Mainline isn't the challenge.
Rhythm control is key.
