# Crew Acquisition, Assignment, and Growth

One core idea:

> **Getting Crew is only the start. Real value comes from correct placement and continued growth.**

If you only recruit them and never organize them, you leave a lot of power on the table.

---

## 1) Understand the two Crew categories first

### Building Crew

Building Crew are assigned to facilities.

Their job is usually to:

- increase production speed
- increase research speed
- improve specific facility efficiency

They are not combat pieces. Their value is long-term mothership output.

### Heroic Crew

Heroic Crew are mainly assigned into the **Echo Hub**.

They provide stat bonuses to matching combat unit styles.

So their value is not just “do you own them,” but whether they are actually integrated into your combat system.

---

## 2) How to get Crew

### Mainline gives your first one early enough

The page states that clearing `Counterattack Operation 8-10` gives your first Crew.

That means:

- new players do not need to wait forever to touch the system
- pushing mainline is part of the Crew unlock path

### Recruitment is the long-term main source

Crew recruitment can provide both:

- Heroic Crew
- Building Crew

The source page says:

- you can recruit with **Deep Space Beacon**
- there are also free pulls over time
- the feature unlocks at **Energy Core Lv.20**

For midgame accounts, this is a slow-build system rather than a one-day completion target.

---

## 3) Growth priorities: understand what each slot is for

### Crew star-ups directly improve value

The page’s practical point is simple:

- Crew use dedicated upgrade materials to raise star level
- stronger stars improve building efficiency or combat bonuses
- new skills are unlocked along the way

You do not need to memorize every material name first.
What matters is the decision rule:

- for Building Crew, ask whether they improve the facilities you use most
- for Heroic Crew, ask whether they strengthen your real main combat setup

### Do not mix building logic and combat logic

Building Crew are long-term snowball tools.

Heroic Crew are more about:

- stronger combat performance
- fuller mainline / PvP formations
- getting more value from additional Echo Hub slots

If you are bottlenecked by research, shipbuilding, or daily resource flow, Building Crew usually give the faster visible gain.

If you are bottlenecked by combat power and roster strength, Heroic Crew + Echo Hub matter more.

---

## 4) Echo Hub is the real multiplier for Heroic Crew

The page explains that once Heroic Crew are unlocked, **Echo Hub** becomes the scaling system around them.

Key points:

- Echo Hub has **10 phases**
- every phase must be fully upgraded before the next one opens
- normal node upgrades use **Echo Module**
- breakthroughs use **Deep Space Echo**

This is not just a side panel. It is the slot and multiplier system for Heroic Crew.

### Direct Echo Hub benefits

- raises matching **Flagship** stats
- increases the number of Heroic Crew you can assign

So the full logic is:

> Recruiting the Crew starts the process. Echo Hub determines how much of that value you actually realize.

---

## 5) Assignment rules matter

### Assign from the Crew page

Open the Crew screen and use the assignment tab to place units into valid slots.

### Building Crew can also be assigned from the facility side

If you already know which facility you want to improve:

1. open the facility
2. choose assignment
3. pick the matching Building Crew

The page also makes clear that not every Crew fits every building.

Examples given include scholars for research-type facilities and technical staff for shipbuilding-related facilities.

The correct mindset is not “fill any empty slot.”
It is **match Crew to facility function**.

---

## 6) Practical order of operations

If you are just starting to organize this system, use this order:

1. push until Crew acquisition is stable
2. never waste free recruitment cycles
3. fill your most-used facilities with Building Crew first
4. then evaluate which Heroic Crew best fit your main combat style
5. keep investing **Echo Module** and **Deep Space Echo** into Echo Hub progression

Short version:

> **Use Building Crew to fix daily efficiency first, then use Heroic Crew to raise your combat ceiling.**

---

## Summary

The Crew system is not mainly about how many you own.
It is about three things:

- separating Building Crew from Heroic Crew
- knowing the two key unlock points: `Counterattack Operation 8-10` and `Energy Core Lv.20`
- using Echo Hub to actually multiply Heroic Crew value

If you have not reviewed your Crew setup recently, the fastest wins are usually:

- fill empty high-importance facility slots first
- then check whether your Echo Hub has unused slots or stalled phase progress
