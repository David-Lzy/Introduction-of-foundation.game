# Store Priorities and Exchange Strategy

This page answers one practical question:

> With so many stores, what should be exchanged first, and what should only be used to patch shortages?

The short version:

- **Take free items first**
- **Check discounted items before full-price ones**
- **Scarce growth materials beat raw water / metal**
- **Don’t let reset-based currencies overflow**

---

## 1) Normal Store: emergency patching only

The Normal Store mainly consumes **Credits**.

### Good use cases

- You are just short of a key building or tech push
- You want a small amount of **Far Voyage Memories** for hero pool progress

### Bad use cases

- Repeatedly spending **Credits** on water / metal as a routine habit

Why:

- **Credits** also accelerate building
- The deeper you go, the more valuable those building accelerations become

---

## 2) Merit Store: free claims first, then manage the monthly cap

### Must-do

- Claim **free items** whenever available
- Review weekly / monthly discounted items first

### Important limit

- On the **1st day of each month at 00:00**, any Merit above **5,000,000** is reset

That means:

- If you are approaching the cap near month-end, spend down below it
- Don’t treat Merit as permanent savings

---

## 3) Glory Store: hero shards first, Flagship blueprints second

If you have stable **Glory Arena** income, a strong default order is:

1. **Legend hero shards**
2. **Flagship blueprints**
3. Lower-impact filler resources

Why:

- Legend shards directly affect roster ceiling
- Flagship blueprints are also a slow, limited-growth resource

---

## 4) Info Store: discounted growth materials first

The **Info Store** spends Info Data.

### Recommended priority

1. Discounted **Legendary Training Manual**
2. Discounted high-value hero shards
3. Food boxes when leveling is the active bottleneck

Best use cases:

- Skill materials are the blocker
- Hero growth is behind schedule
- You are timing a growth push around an event milestone

If you are not under pressure, don’t dump all Info Data casually.

---

## 5) Commerce Guild Store: keep the long-term value logic

This mostly aligns with the existing guild chapter, but can be tightened into a clearer order.

### Stable priority order

1. **Deep Space Beacon**
2. Daily / weekly free items
3. Your current bottleneck resources
4. **AP potions** when you expect heavy rally / request usage
5. **Echo Module / Deep Space Echo** once your account is actually at that stage

### Common mistake

- Buying discounted water / metal automatically while skipping rarer weekly items

Water and metal gradually become easier to replace.
**Deep Space Beacon**, AP-related value, and late growth materials do not.

---

## 6) A practical exchange order

### Newer / lighter players

- Free items
- **Deep Space Beacon**
- Discounted growth materials
- Urgent bottleneck resources

### Mid / late-game active players

- Free items
- **Deep Space Beacon / Legendary Training Manual / high-value shards**
- Flagship blueprints
- AP potions and late growth materials
- Generic resource patching last

---

## 7) Weekly self-check

Once a week, review:

1. Which currency is closest to a cap or reset point?
2. Are there high-value discounts this week?
3. Are you bottlenecked by raw resources, skill materials, or recruitment / growth materials?
4. Did you exchange a high-value currency into a low-value resource out of habit?

---

## Summary

A strong store routine is not “buy whatever is available.”
It is:

- claim free value first
- buy scarce value first
- solve reset-risk currencies first
- patch raw resources only at real bottlenecks

That way, the same currency turns into more long-term account speed.
