# PvP Quick Reference Card

Use this page as a fast pre-fight and pre-logout checklist.

---

## Do not attack

- MIT guild ships
- Cargo ships
- Ships involved in rallies
- Ships involved in events
- Any target in non-PvP zones

---

## Unsafe zones

These star levels are treated as PvP / unsafe:

- Star 4
- Star 6
- Star 7
- Star 8

If you enter them, assume combat is possible.

---

## Before attacking

Ask yourself:

1. Is this target an ally?
2. Is this a cargo ship?
3. Is it doing a rally or event task?
4. Am I in a legal PvP zone?

If you are not sure, do not hit first.

---

## Before going offline

- Recall fleets to base
- Do not leave vulnerable fleets in unsafe zones

---

## Guild expectations

- Read announcements
- Watch guild comms
- Reply when leadership DMs you
- Participate in events when possible

---

## One-line rule

**When in doubt, avoid the attack and re-check first.**
