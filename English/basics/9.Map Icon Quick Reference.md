# Map Icon Quick Reference

This page does not try to explain the whole map system.
It only turns the most common icons into a fast checklist.

If you open the map and hesitate about what to tap first, use this page.

---

## 1. Space map: separate "free pickup", "fly there first", and "this starts a fight"

### Green hand
- no need to move right on top of it
- can be collected directly
- usually gives basic resources like **Water** and **Metal**

### Pickaxe
- you must fly to it first
- also gives **Water** and **Metal**
- color changes the amount:
  - **blue**: 5,000
  - **purple**: 10,000
  - **gold**: 20,000

If time is tight, prioritize the higher-color nodes.

### Question mark
- may trigger a fight
- may also reveal a new planet

Treat it as an exploration point, not a pure resource node.

### Skull
- starts a **space battle**
- winning gives normal rewards like resources and Food

### Treasure chest
- direct pickup
- often includes Hero shards and resources

### Ascendancy target / roaming elite icon
- starts a stronger PvE encounter
- higher levels are harder
- rewards include resource drops and Food

### Tribute Vessel
- better handled through a **rally**
- difficulty rises with level
- rewards lean more toward long-term materials such as blueprints

---

## 2. Planet surface: check whether it is a war node, a port node, or an exploration entry

### War Declaration
- used by alliance leadership to start the attack flow
- winning leads to control of the target port

### New Port
- can be set as your **Home Port** after moving in
- severe damage can be repaired inside its range
- defeated fleets return there

So New Port is not just a marker.
It is also a recovery and staging point.

### Explore
- enters the planet's internal content
- may lead to ground battle, space battle, a Boss, events, or a shop

On a fresh planet, this is usually where your efficient cleanup starts.

---

## 3. Inside a planet: clear normal nodes before touching the Boss

The source page ends with a very practical rule:

> **When you reach a new planet, tap the non-Boss icons first.**

Why:

- you collect resources first
- you may open shops or event rewards first
- you often become stronger before the Boss check

### Common internal icons

#### Gun
- **ground battle**
- fights enemies inside that area
- rewards are usually resource-oriented

#### Skull
- **space battle**
- fought with your fleet
- rewards are also regular resource rewards

#### Speech bubble
- event choice node
- rewards depend on the option taken

#### Shop
- spend **Galactic Coin** here

#### Boss
- may be a ground battle or a space battle
- reward is usually better
- but it is usually not the first thing you should tap

---

## 4. The most useful map-check order

When you open the map, check in this order:

1. direct pickups like **green hands** and **chests**
2. higher-value **pickaxe** nodes
3. the **space battle / ground battle** nodes you already planned to clear today
4. on a new planet, clear **non-Boss** content first
5. if you need a long-term staging point, evaluate **New Port / Home Port** position

---

## One-line takeaway

> **Read map icons as free pickup, travel node, or combat node first; on a new planet, clear normal nodes before the Boss.**
